Falafel and Hummus Recipe

Falafel

Ingredients:-

  1. Soaked chickpeas
  2. Garlic
  3. Onion
  4. Red chilli powder
  5. Baking Soda
  6. Corriander chopped
  7. Salt

Procedure

  • Except chopped corriander grind all the ingredients together and make a soft fine paste.
  • Add chopped corriander to the mixture and mix well. Now make small size or accoourding to your desire balls of all the mixture.
  • Now deep fry the balls and falafel are ready to eat.

Hummus

Ingredients:-

  1. Soaked and boiled chickpeas
  2. Tahini
  3. Cumin powder
  4. Salt
  5. Lemon juice
  6. Garlic

Procedure:-

  • Grind all together and make a fine paste.
  • If needed add some water to dilute it, pour it in a serving bowl , add olove oil for better taste.
